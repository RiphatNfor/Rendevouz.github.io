You are welcome to our online Patient/Doctor booking plantform. This is design to facilitate your appointment with different doctors and hospitals of your choice around Bamenda. We've included a bunch of different hospitals in our booking platform to enable you make the best of choice.

How To Book An Appointment?

Step 1

On the home page, fill in the information as required in the form. When done, select the dropdown menu below the form and choose a desired hospital of your choice. When you must have selected a desired hospital of your choice, click the Book button below the menu to start your appoinment proceedure need help? let us know

Step 2

From here, you'll be taken to another page, where you'll have to search for available appointment at a given moment. You'll have to search either by Date or by the Doctor availbale at that moment. when you have completed the selection, click the Proceed button to complete you appointment. Wait for a moment, a message will be sent to your email You can also decide to Cancel your appointment, if you wish or you've made a mistake and wish to start all over again.
